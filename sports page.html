Max Verstappen wins the Monza Grand Prix and the fastest time.


Published by: Ryzl James Balindres

But, it wasn't easy or just luck for Max to win, he had to defend and overtake very well. Max also has to fight other more experienced drivers such as Lewis Hamilton and Fernando Alonso for the lead.

Currently, Mclaren is winning the constructors championship, while 2 drivers battling for the drivers championship. But redbull is second on the constructors, while Max being 3rd on the drivers championship. For Max to win against better cars is a challenge and amazing to watch
