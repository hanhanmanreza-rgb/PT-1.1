Youtuber "Schlep" gets a cease and desist letter from roblox.


Published by Johann Arvin Manreza


Famous youtuber "Schlep" recently received a cease and desist letter from roblox due to catching predators" on their platform.

Schlep calls the cease and desist letter unfair and stupid for letting predators. Freely roam about the platform. After catching about 4+ predators on the platform. Roblox deems him a threat to the platform" due to him being a vigilante. Which he is not as be legally brings matters into his own hands without breaking the law. Shlep has reported to be seen talking to multiple lawyers about the case.

Due to this situation, multiple countries has recently banned Roblox. As well as this multiple game developers on Roblox left the platform. To this day Roblox still denies that what Shlep is doing is right
