Students should be allowed to leave school on their own freedom or responsibility.


Published by: Zoe Maxeane Maquilan


Yes because it is about the responsibility. Ateneo students these days would be less dependent if given more freedom and responsibility taking steps to do more and be more for their parents on July 1, 2025.

Parents should let their children be responsible in getting home if they cannot find a way for them. Every young student who struggles to get home have the same problem in the biggest step which is to leave the campus. They can do it by commuting or ordering an exit pass. Public transportation has been a solution in getting around the busy city of cebu. Students should learn on how to utilize these as this is a sign of their maturity and responsibility.

In conclusion, students should learn from a young age to take responsibility for their school and parents take for them, be independent.
