Duck, cover, and hold on! For this year’s Ateneo earthquake evacuation drill will take place on August 29, 2025 Friday.


Published by Stan Anthony Ybanez


Last week friday, Ateneo Hearters had a school event of their their practice evacuation drill. It happened in Ateneo’s track and field where they do certain tasks to keep a safe distance and organized formation.

This evacuation drill included all SHS-ADC students from nursery to senior high school. This procedure has not only lead by the faculty and staff but also student leaders. Leaders for RYC, REAVO, Magis responders, scouts, and leaders council.

Fire fighters a,and other medical personnel were there to help too. Instructors were told by Ateneo Hearters on how to handle gear incase of an emergency. The instructions insured that the students were safe and that they were all at the oval.
